The Problem

Our Solution

Smart Segregation

Visually divide your account balance into purpose-specific segments without creating separate accounts.

Instant Access

Keep funds accessible while still mentally allocating them for specific purposes.

User-Friendly Interface

Simple mobile-first design that makes financial planning intuitive and visual.
